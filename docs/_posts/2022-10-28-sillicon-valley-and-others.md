# Biggest Difference Between Silicon Valley and Others

## Intro
Silicon Valley is the center.  
How?

## Center of Gravity
Others in the world view Sillicon Valley or some other entity as the leading figure and the center of gravity in the industry or technology, and thus try to benchmark them.

People in Sillicon Valley idenfity themselves as the center, unknowingly, and don't really care what the status quo or other are doing because they believe what they will do will eventually be the center of gravity in their field. 

Taking chances, new ideas/products, boldness. All comes from this.
